How Used

Its a bit harder to see how repetition is utalized on this page because one really can see it when they scroll though the website. If you were to click on the link on the bottom of this page you would be directed to the site, and then you would be able to see that almost all the important parts of repetition are being utalized on eacxh page. The colors, headings, line-spacing, navigation menu, and the footer information all are the same.
