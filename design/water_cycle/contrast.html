How Used

The aspect of contrast is really well done in this screenshot as well This is shown in the middle section, with the large, small, and then large again boxes showing the diffrent stories therein. The page also makes it easy for the eyes to flow across the page and look at everything on it. Another way contrast is being used is by the fact the page is almost full of the colors of blue, white, or black. Its also interesting to note that the smaller box in the middle of the page is also written in a diffrent text color of black, rather than white, really making it pop out even more than normal.
